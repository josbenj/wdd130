Explore Our Trips

Apurimac River

Duration: 3 days / 2 nights

Level: Intermediate to Advanced (Class III–V)

Description: Navigate deep canyons and wild rapids through one of the most scenic rivers in Peru. Ideal for thrill-seekers ready for a full adventure.

Itinerary: Includes riverside camping, daily rafting stages, and meals in the wilderness.

Urubamba River

Duration: Half-day or full-day

Level: Beginner to Intermediate (Class II–III)

Description: Paddle through the Sacred Valley with scenic views of Inca ruins and Andean peaks. Great for families and first-timers.

Itinerary: Morning rafting with optional lunch stop and return to Cusco by afternoon.

Cañete River

Duration: 1 day

Level: Beginner to Intermediate (Class III)

Description: A fun and fast-paced river just a few hours from Lima. Ideal for quick getaways, groups, or first rafting experiences.

Itinerary: Morning safety briefing, guided descent, and optional wine tasting nearby.
